## Purpose

The Sub-Chapter Framework Working Group (SCFWG) is established to design and recommend a standardized framework for creating and managing sub-chapters under MENACOSE within the MENA region. The framework will define objectives, governance, roles, and operational rules to ensure alignment with INCOSEâ€™s vision while addressing regional diversity.
## Objectives

- Define the mission and objectives of MENACOSE sub-chapters.  
- Establish governance and reporting structures between chapter and sub-chapters.  
- Develop criteria for forming, approving, and sustaining sub-chapters.  
- Provide guidance on leadership, elections, and member engagement.  
- Outline rules for financial management, events, and knowledge-sharing.  
- Recommend inclusive and equitable practices across the region.
## Deliverables

- Draft Sub-Chapter Framework (v0.1) for feedback.
- Final Sub-Chapter Framework Handbook (v1.0) approved by the MENACOSE Board and INCOSE HQ.
- Pilot implementation report capturing lessons learned.  
- Templates and guidelines for proposals, elections, reporting, and events.
## Membership & Governance

- Open to all MENACOSE members in good standing.
- Dr Maged Elaasar is the WG Chair, supported by Dr Bassem Hassan as Deputy Chair(s) and Hassan Eldeeb as Secretary.
- Operates under the authority of the MENACOSE Chapter Board.
- Decisions by consensus, or majority vote when necessary.
- Monthly meetings; quarterly progress reports to the Board.
## Timeline

- Month 1: Establish WG, review best practices.
- Month 2: Draft framework (v0.1).
- Month 3: Member feedback and revisions.
- Month 4: Pilot implementation for one quarter.
- Month 7: Final Handbook (v1.0) and pilot report.
## Success Criteria

- Board approval of the Sub-Chapter Framework.  
- Successful launch of at least one pilot sub-chapter.
- Increased engagement and geographic representation across the region.
